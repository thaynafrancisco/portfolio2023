Stap - Task Management

Our goal is clear - build a responsive task management system that didn't just meet but anticipated the unique needs of your team. Think of it as a hub where efficiency and collaboration converge.

Crafting Solutions:

From the get-go, responsiveness was a non-negotiable. Whether you're at your desk or on the move, the user experience remains seamless. Employee registration became a breeze, simplifying the onboarding process. Task creation? We made it intuitive and collaborative, fostering a sense of shared responsibility.

Facing Challenges Head-On:

Task management pains are universal. Our project directly tackled the common hurdles – lack of visibility, communication gaps, and delays. We didn't just build a tool; we crafted a solution to transform how your team collaborates and executes tasks.

Divam - Landing Page Usability Improvement

Divam presented an exciting challenge – to elevate the user experience on their landing page. As the lead product designer, I embarked on a mission to transform a digital space into a captivating and intuitive portal for users interested in exploring the world of online psychology services.

Solutions Crafted with Precision

Our approach was meticulous. We scrutinized every element, ensuring clarity and purpose. The design solutions implemented not only refined the visual aesthetics but also streamlined the user journey, making it a seamless navigation experience.

Addressing Pain Points

Understanding user pain points was central to our strategy. Through careful analysis and user feedback, we identified areas of friction and ambiguity. The goal was to eradicate these pain points and pave the way for an engaging and frustration-free interaction.

Refmed - Patient Referral Management Software

I am proud to showcase my involvement in the development of Refmed, a cutting-edge patient referral management software. Refmed serves as a robust network for medical professionals, facilitating seamless referral and counter-referral processes. Esteemed doctors leverage this platform to manage relationships effectively and enhance their patient influx.

Key Contributions:

Feature Enhancement: As a key contributor to the project, I played a pivotal role in enhancing crucial features. This includes the implementation of a streamlined system for adding additional consultation addresses and extra secretarial support, providing users with unprecedented flexibility in their practice management. User-Centric Design: I actively participated in crafting a user-centric design, ensuring that medical professionals experience an intuitive and efficient platform. The goal was to create an interface that not only meets the complex needs of healthcare providers but also enhances the overall patient care experience.
